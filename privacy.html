Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 8, 2025

Welcome to DEBoot! We prioritize your privacy and are committed to protecting your data. This policy outlines how we handle your information.

1. Data Collection

We collect only what’s necessary:

  • Chat History: Stored locally in your browser using localStorage for a seamless experience.
  • No Personal Info: We do not collect personal information unless you voluntarily provide it within chat messages or uploaded files.

2. Data Usage

Your data enhances your experience:

  • Context: Used to maintain coherent conversations by referencing past interactions.
  • No Sharing: We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data stays on your device:

  • Local: Stored in your browser’s localStorage for accessibility across sessions.
  • Cloud: Optional backups may occur if you use Puter’s cloud services, governed by their privacy terms.

4. File Uploads

When you upload files:

  • Processing: Files (text, PDFs, images) are processed locally or via Puter’s API for analysis.
  • Temporary Storage: Image files may be temporarily stored in PutWANer’s cloud for AI vision processing, then deleted.
